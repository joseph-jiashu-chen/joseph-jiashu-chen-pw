Regional High School Model United Nations Conference 2014 - Security Council

The main objective of Security Council for this conference was to achieve resolutions revolving around the issue of mitigating sectarian violence in Pakistan. Representing the nation of Nigeria, my partner and I were able to establish a resolution alongside several of our fellow Security Council nations that had a streamlined approach on several important factors while respecting Pakistan's Sovereignty: Civilian Security, Sub-national Counterterrorism Development, Judicial Reform, Educational Reform and Organization, Health Regulation, and Religious Unification. While many of these factors may seem to have no correlation to Sectarian Violence based on first glance, all of the points mentioned do in fact tie back to Sectarian Violence. Several examples are the development of unregistered madrassas with curriculum intended to fuel sectarian violence on a local level, and the establishments of Sharia Courts with overall biased rulings against religious denominations.

Santa Clara Valley Model United Nations Conference 2015 - World Bank

First off, being Vice President of our MUN team at the time, not only did I compete in this conference, but alongside our president and the rest of the secretariat, we put together this conference that hosted over 700 people. For the committee, we were assigned the difficult task of efficiently financing disaster relief. Representing Serbia, alongside several other nations, we came to establish a resolution of establishing micro-insurance and index-based insurance programs to cover developing third world nations, fostering public-private partnerships in the area of infrastructure protection, and utilizing established database systems to record loss and provide reasonable damage assessment based on geographical conditions and circumstances of disasters.

Stanford Model United Nations Conference 2014 - Cabinet of Park Chung Hee

In perhpas my craziest crisis committee ever, I represented the Minister of Unification in a simulation of the Cabinet of Park Chung Hee in the year 1972. Dealing with all sorts of ongoing problems and crises in South Korea at the time, I was able to secretly unify, with North Korea's consent, South Korea and North Korea once again. About to enter an age of prosperity in Korea, the Crisis directors of the committee decided to assassinate the Minister of Unification. I was then reborn as the US Ambassador to the South Korean Cabinet, and the moments of peace with South Korea and North Korea ended as American intervention, led by the US Ambassador, began in South Korea. The Prime Minister directed a coup and assassinated President Park Chung Hee(Committee Head Chair), and became president of South Korea.

Berkeley Model United Nations Conference 2015 - Security COuncil

At one of the most prestigious west coast Model United Nations Conferences, I was able to represent Lithuania in Security Council and fortunate enough to win 3rd place in a field of elite MUNers. The topic of this committee was the prevention of the rapidly expanding Islamic State. Despite split opinions regarding the level of intervention that should be performed and the necessary military actions that should and should not be executed, our resolution group was able to come to an overall consensus. Lithuania's role in the resolution focused on the economic impact and dependency of the Islamic State, military and humanitarian aid in affected regions, military action via a UN led coalition, and the Islamic State's propaganda and recruitment.

Santa Clara Valley Model United Nations Conference 2014 - UNHCR

In my second model united nations conference ever, my partner and I were able to represent Vietnam in two serious ongoing humanitarian crises. The first topic revolved around the issues of the refugees from the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The second topic focused on the refugee crisis from Syria. Both refugee crises were caused mainly due to the militant extremist groups within the respective nations. Overall, the objective was to reform the refugee camps in the neighboring nations so that the refugees could eventually return to their nation or gradually establish themselves in the new nation's society.

Inventory Reference Counting

This code utilizes linked lists and reference counting to track the inventory of items using what could be considered as a 4 digit barcode. The code will provide a report on the status of the items by taking in commands that result in the linked list node for the item number increasing or decreasing its count. Inadequate commands will also be accounted for in the report of the inventory.

Spell Checker

Starting with a "dictionary" file of 23907 words, the code dynamically allocates memory in order to read the 23907 words into an array of strings. The words are then sorted so that when another file containing a passage is read into the code, the passage can be parsed into individual words and through a binary search of the array of strings with the 23907 words, the words of the passage can be checked for spelling by noting whether the word is present in the 23907 words.

  • Date: October 2014
